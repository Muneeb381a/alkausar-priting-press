— How it started

Our Story

Alkausar Printing Press started in a small workshop with one offset press and a big dream. Our founders shared a single vision: to bring industrial-grade precision to every project, no matter the size.

Over the years, we've grown into a full-service printing house with cutting-edge equipment — but our core remains unchanged. Meticulous attention to detail, respect for deadlines, and an unwavering commitment to quality.

Today, we serve clients from local startups to international brands, yet we still treat every job as if it were our first.
