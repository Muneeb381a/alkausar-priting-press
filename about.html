Our story

Alkausar Printing Press started in a small workshop with one offset press and a big dream. Founders Abdul Rahman and Khalid Ansari shared a vision: to bring industrial-grade precision to every project, no matter the size.

Over 25 years, we've grown into a full-service printing house with cutting-edge equipment, but our core remains unchanged — meticulous attention to detail, respect for deadlines, and an unwavering commitment to quality. Today, we serve clients from startups to Fortune 500 companies, yet we still treat every job as if it were our first.
